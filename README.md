Sales Performance Analysis This project analyzes sales data to identify trends across regions and categories.

Dataset: Includes 3,000+ transaction records with sales and profit metrics.

Key Tool: Utilizes Excel Pivot Tables for rapid data aggregation.

Top Category: Electronics is the highest-grossing category.

Top Region: The West region leads in total revenue generation.

Growth Area: South region identified as the lowest-performing territory.

Best Segment: Corporate clients yield higher sales than individual consumers.

Underperformers: Clothing and Books require strategy shifts to improve margins.

Dashboard: Features a visual summary for tracking KPIs at a glance.

Goal: To provide data-driven recommendations for increasing profitability.

Files: Contains Raw Data, Pivot Reports, Dashboards, and Insights.
